# Vantage User Guide

Vantage is a simulated stock trading platform for students and teachers. All portfolios use virtual money — no real funds are ever at risk.

---

## For Students

### 1. Sign In

- Open Vantage in your browser.
- Click **Sign in with Google** to use your Google account, or enter your email and password if you registered manually.
- If you forgot your password, click **Forgot password?** and follow the link sent to your email.

---

### 2. Build Your Portfolio

**Add a stock**
1. Use the search bar at the top of any page to look up a stock by name or ticker symbol (e.g. "AAPL" or "Apple").
2. Click the stock to open its detail page.
3. Click **Add to Portfolio**, enter the number of shares and your average cost, then confirm.

**Update or remove a holding**
1. Go to **Portfolio** in the left sidebar.
2. Find the stock you want to change and click **Edit**.
3. Adjust the shares and average cost, then save — or click **Remove** to delete the holding entirely.

---

### 3. Track Your Watchlist

**Add a stock to watch**
1. Open any stock's detail page via the search bar.
2. Click the **star icon** (☆) near the stock name to add it to your watchlist.

**View your watchlist**
1. Click **Watchlist** in the left sidebar.
2. Stocks are listed with their current price and daily change. Click any row to open the stock detail page.

**Remove from watchlist**
1. On the Watchlist page, click the star icon next to any stock to remove it.

---

### 4. Check Your Standing on the Leaderboard

1. Click **Leaderboard** in the left sidebar.
2. You'll see rankings for your class, sorted by portfolio return percentage from when you joined.
3. Use the tabs at the top to switch between your **Class**, your **State**, or the **National** leaderboard.

---

### 5. Post and Browse Trading Ideas

**Post an idea**
1. Click **Ideas** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **New Idea**.
3. Enter the ticker symbol, choose **BUY** or **SELL**, set an entry price, a target price, and a timeframe in days.
4. Add a short rationale explaining your thinking, then click **Post**.

**React to an idea**
- Click the 👍 button on any idea card to like it. Click again to remove your like.

**Delete your own idea**
- Click the **trash icon** on one of your own idea cards to delete it.

---

### 6. Follow Class Activity

1. Click **Activity** in the left sidebar.
2. The **My Activity** tab shows your own recent trades and portfolio changes in order.
3. Switch to the **Class** tab to see what your classmates have been doing.
4. In the Class tab, use the **Timeline / By Student** toggle to switch between a chronological feed and a view grouped by person.
5. If you're in more than one class, use the class selector dropdown to switch between them.

---

### 7. Explore Related Stocks

When viewing any stock's detail page, scroll down to the **Also held by classmates** widget. It shows other stocks that your classmates hold alongside this one, ranked by how many people hold both. Click any pill to jump to that stock's detail page.

---

### 8. Join or Create a Peer Group

Peer groups let you compete with friends outside your formal class.

**Create a group**
1. Click **My Groups** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Create Group**.
3. Give your group a name, an optional description, and a starting balance.
4. Click **Create** — your group's join code (e.g. `BULL-7X3K`) is shown on the group card. Share it with friends.

**Join a group**
1. Click **My Groups** in the left sidebar.
2. Click **Join Group**.
3. Enter the join code your friend shared with you and click **Join**.

**Inside a group**
- The **Leaderboard** tab ranks all members by portfolio return since joining.
- The **Activity** tab shows recent trades by group members.
- Your join code is always visible on the group card — click the copy icon to copy it to your clipboard.

---

### 9. View Trade History

1. Click **History** in the left sidebar.
2. Your full log of portfolio changes is shown in reverse chronological order, including what was bought or sold, at what price, and when.

---

## For Teachers

Teachers have two modes accessible via the **Switch to Teaching / Switch to Trading** button at the bottom of the left sidebar.

- **Teaching mode** — focused on managing your class and monitoring students.
- **Trading mode** — the same view as a student, so you can demonstrate trades or participate alongside your class.

---

### 1. Apply for Teacher Access

If you don't yet have a teacher account:

1. Sign in with any account.
2. Click **Teach Here** (if visible) or navigate to the Classroom page.
3. Fill in your title, school name, state, and school website.
4. Submit your application. An admin will review it and you'll receive an email when it's approved or if more information is needed.

---

### 2. Create a Class

1. Switch to **Teaching mode** using the toggle at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. Click **My Classes** in the sidebar.
3. Click **Create Class**.
4. Enter the class name, school name, state, starting balance, start date, and an optional end date.
5. Toggle **Ideas public** on if you want student ideas to appear on the state/national feed.
6. Click **Create**.

---

### 3. Invite Students

1. Open the class from **My Classes**.
2. Click **Invite Students**.
3. Enter one or more student email addresses (comma-separated).
4. Click **Send Invites** — each student receives an email with a join link. The link is valid for 7 days.

---

### 4. Monitor the Leaderboard

1. Click **Leaderboard** in the sidebar (Teaching mode).
2. Your class leaderboard shows every enrolled student ranked by their portfolio return percentage since joining.
3. Students who haven't made any trades will appear at the bottom with 0% return.

---

### 5. Review Student Activity

1. Click **Activity** in the sidebar (Teaching mode).
2. Select your class from the dropdown if you manage more than one.
3. Use the **Timeline** view for a chronological feed of all student trades, or switch to **By Student** to see each person's activity grouped together.
4. Students with no recent entries are easy to spot in the By Student view.

---

### 6. Moderate Trading Ideas

1. Click **Ideas** in the sidebar.
2. Browse ideas posted by students in your class.
3. Click the **trash icon** on any idea to remove it if it's inappropriate or off-topic.

---

### 7. Switch to Trading Mode

To demonstrate a trade or participate as a student:

1. Click **Switch to Trading** at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. You now see the full student interface — Dashboard, Portfolio, Watchlist, History, and so on.
3. Click **Switch to Teaching** at any time to return to the teacher view.

---

## Tips

- All prices and portfolio values are based on real market data but no real money is involved.
- Leaderboard rankings are based on **percentage return** from your starting balance, not absolute value — so a smaller starting balance doesn't put you at a disadvantage.
- Trading ideas track their own outcome automatically — once the timeframe expires the result (hit, missed, or expired) is recorded.
- Peer group join codes never expire, so you can share them anytime.
